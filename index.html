Using the mouse-wheel you can zoom in and out. By draging with your primary click, one can move the coordinate plane along the real axis.

We recommend increasing the level \(N\) not too high. For \(\Gamma_0(N)\) levels up to 2000 work quite fast. For \(\Gamma_1(N)\) we can go up to 200 without problems.
When using \(\Gamma(N)\) we see a big decrease in performance at levels above 20. The algorithm for finding the coset-representatives in this case are not optimized at all.
